Plug in and play

Connect your MIDI keyboard via USB — the app detects it automatically. No setup, no drivers, no configuration. Just plug in and start playing.

See what you play

Every key lights up the moment you press it. Play softly for a gentle glow, play hard for full color. Your student sees exactly what your fingers are doing.

Animation: hands playing a phrase, keys respond with velocity
Animation: webcam + piano window side by side in a call

Show it in a video call

Share the piano window in Zoom, Google Meet, or Discord. Your student sees both your hands and the keys on screen. Read our setup guide for step-by-step instructions.

Mark any key

Toggle the pencil tool to draw on any key. Mark a scale, a chord, or finger positions for your student. Right-click to erase. Works with mouse or MIDI input.

Animation: pencil cursor marking a C major scale

Save and share

Take a screenshot with one click. Send it in chat, paste it into a worksheet, or save it for later. The image shows exactly what's on screen — marked keys, note names, everything.

Built-in sound

No speakers or external software needed. Turn on the built-in piano sound and hear what you play through your computer. Great for quick practice or when you don't have headphones handy.

Make it yours

Pick your highlight color, show or hide note names, choose sharp or flat notation, adjust the keyboard range from 1 to 7 octaves. Available in 8 languages.

Free, no catch

Open source, no subscription, no ads, no account needed. Works completely offline. Anyone can read the code, suggest improvements, or translate it into their language.
